A stochastic process is an infinite collection of random variables . Martingales are a specific type of stochastic process that are used in finance. The basic idea is that a martingale is stochastic process where, under a certain type of probability measure, the expected value of the random variable in the future given all present and past information, is the value of the random variable in the present.
